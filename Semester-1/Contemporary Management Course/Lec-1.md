# Lecture #1: Managerial Functions & Skills 

### Management Functions and Skills

* **Manager Levels**
  * First line managers
  * Middle layer Managers (Department lead)
  * Baord level - Decision makers
* **Manager main Function**
  * *Planning*
  * *Organizing*
    * Decision making (centralized vs decentralized)
    * Handhover and sync
    * define *SOP* (Standard operating Procedure)
  * *Leading*
    * Motivating Employees
    * Resolving conflicts among members
    * Selecting Most Effective Comm channels
  * *Controlling*
    * Monitoring
    * comparing
    * Correcting: only in case of deviation from the standards.
* **Managerial Skills**
  * *Technical Skills*: Knowledge & Proficiency ina specific field  ➡️ *First...Middle* - Manager
  * *Human Skills*: ability to work well with other people ➡️ *First & Middle & Top* - Manager
  * *Conceptual Skills*: ability to think and conceptualize about abstract and complex situation concerning the org. ➡️ *Middle & Top* - Manger
  * Essential Workplace Skills:
    * Critical thinking
    * Teamwork skills
    * **Adaptability** ➡️ Most important one
      * Without this, then u r in the path to lose ur competitive-edge = Lose Market.
    * Digital Literacy
    * Creativity
    * leadership Skills
    * Communication Skills
      * Comm with Top-Manag: Simple, concise comm - fact based
      * Comm with lower-Manag: elaborate comm and in-details
    * Problem-solving Skills
    * Time Management
* **Managerial Roles**
  * *Interpersonal Roles*: Figurehead, leader, ***liaison*** (relationship oriented)
  * *Informational Roles*: Monitor, Disseminator, Spokesperson
  * *Decisional Roles*: Disturbance-handler, Resource-allocator, Negotiator, Entrepreneurs
